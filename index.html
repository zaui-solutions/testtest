Expedition Overview

Join us for a challenging 7-day high-altitude traverse across the majestic Sentry and Mount Olive peaks. This expedition covers approximately 2,500m of vertical gain and requires participants to be equipped with expert-level mountaineering skills, including technical ice climbing proficiency. Safety protocols and gear checks are mandatory.

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Basecamp Setup and Orientation (Elevation: 1,800m)
  • Day 2: Ascent to Sentry's Ridge (High-Wind Potential)
  • Day 3: Technical Traverse to Mt. Olive West Face (Ice climbing required)
  • Day 4: Summit Day (Mt. Olive, 3,250m) & Descent to Camp Delta
  • Day 5: Recovery and Environmental Study
  • Day 6: Final Descent Stage I (River and Canyon Crossing)
  • Day 7: Trek to Extraction Point & Departure
